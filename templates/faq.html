AskLASA FAQ

What kind of stuff can I ask here?
Honestly, pretty much anything related to LASA courses, life, and everything in between. I ask that you keep conversations respectful and inquisitive - this isn't a place to spread rumors or make fun of others.
I'm not attending LASA. Can I still participate in the forum?
As long as you're not impersonating a student here or arguing that your school is better, that's totally fine! You can learn about what we do here and share your non-LASA experiences. If you are a prospective LASA student in middle school, this is a great way to get firsthand information.
I'm a parent of a student. Can I ask questions here?
This was designed to be for students, so I don't really encourage it. A better way to gain information and insight would be to contact an administrator via email, or to visit the official LASA website here.
Is this the main LASA home page?
No, it's not. You can find the main LASA website here. This site is supported by LASA, though, so I guess it's kind of official?
Where do I report bugs or inappropriate behavior?
For any technical site issues, you can email me here, and I'll try my best to fix it. The more descriptive your problem, the better.

However, for any offensive conduct, I recommend you take that up with a counselor or school administrator. Not that I don't want to help (and you can tell me too, I don't judge!), but I can't really do much.
